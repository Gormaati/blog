Introduction to the Banjara Community

English

The Banjara community, also known as the Lamani or Lambadi, is a traditionally nomadic ethnic group that resides primarily in the states of Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh in India. The Banjara people are known for their vibrant culture, distinctive clothing, and rich traditions.
